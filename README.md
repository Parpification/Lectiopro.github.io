# Privacy Policy

**Lectio Pro Browser Extension**
Last updated: April 17, 2025

---

## Overview

Lectio Pro is a browser extension that enhances the Lectio school platform. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, and what rights you have as a user.

Lectio Pro is committed to your privacy. The extension does **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) — no names, email addresses, usernames, IP addresses, or any data that could be used to identify you as an individual.

---

## What Data Is Collected

Lectio Pro may collect **non-identifiable usage data**, which may include:

- Which features are enabled or disabled (e.g. Palette, Alias, Pulse)
- Interaction patterns with extension UI elements (e.g. which panels are opened)
- Error and crash reports
- General performance metrics (e.g. load times)
- Browser type and version

This data is **aggregate and anonymous** by design. It cannot be linked back to any individual user.

---

## What Data Is NOT Collected

Lectio Pro does **not** collect:

- Your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers
- Your Lectio username or school affiliation
- Your schedule, assignments, grades, messages, or any content from Lectio
- Your IP address or geolocation
- Cookies or authentication tokens
- Browsing history outside of the Lectio platform

---

## How Data Is Used

Any non-identifiable data collected is used solely to:

- Understand how features are being used in order to improve the extension
- Identify and fix bugs or performance issues
- Inform future development priorities

Data is **never** sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

---

## Data Storage and Retention

Non-identifiable usage data may be stored temporarily for analysis purposes and is deleted on a rolling basis. No data is stored locally on your device beyond what is necessary for the extension to function (e.g. your saved preferences via `chrome.storage.local`).

Your local extension preferences (such as theme settings or enabled features) are stored **only on your own device** using the browser's built-in storage API and are never transmitted.

---

## Third-Party Services

Lectio Pro uses **Google Analytics** to collect anonymous usage data. Google Analytics may collect information such as:

- Pages or views within the extension
- Feature interactions and events
- Browser type and version
- General session information (e.g. session duration)

Google Analytics does **not** receive any personally identifiable information from Lectio Pro. IP anonymization is enabled, meaning your IP address is truncated before any data is stored by Google.

Google's data collection is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites and extensions using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

Lectio Pro does not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---
